In Scope: The Cheesecake Factory Inc

Headquartered in Calabasas Hills, California, The Cheeesecake Factory operates a chain of restaurants through multiple brands, including The Cheeesecake Factory, North Italia, Other FRC, and Other segments. Its namesake segment features a comprehensive menu of appetizers, small plates, steaks, seafood, chicken, burgers, pastas, and salads, as well as over 45 premium cheesecake varieties. The North Italia brand specializes in Italian cuisine, while the Other FRC segment includes brands acquired from Fox Restaurant Concepts. In fiscal year 2023, the company had annual revenues of $3.44 billion.
